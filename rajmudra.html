"प्रतिपच्चन्द्रलेखेव वर्धिष्णुर्विश्ववंदिता, शाहसूनोः शिवस्यैषा मुद्रा भद्राय राजते।"

"Shivaji Maharaj Rajmudra" Is The Official Stamp Of Shivaji Maharaj Which Was Used On His Official
Letters,Orders Etc. Rajmudra Was Also "Royal Coin" During The Reign Of ‘Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’

Below The Details Of Text Mentioned In Shivaji Maharaj Seal With Explanations:

Sanskrit: प्रतिपच्चन्द्रलेखेव वर्धिष्णुर्विश्ववन्दिता |
शाहसूनो: शिवस्यैषा मुद्रा भद्राय राजते ||
Hindi: जैसे-जैसे प्रतिपदा का चाँद बढ़ता है, और जैसा कि सारी दुनिया उसे
नमस्कार करती है, वैसे ही यह मुद्रा और उसका लौकिक उदय होगा ।
English: This Seal Of Shivaji, The Son Of Shahaji, Increasing In Size Like The Moon Of Pratipada (The First Day After The Moonless Night), It Will Be Worshiped By The World & It Will Shine Only For Well Being Of People. Now You Will Notice The Provincal Emblem Of Maharashtra Government Is Almost Same Text On Seal With A Change In Third Line Of Verse. That Is "शाहसूनो: शिवस्यैषा" Is Replaced With "महाराष्ट्रस्य राज्यस्य" प्रतिपच्चंद्रलेखेव वर्धिष्णुर्विश्व वंदिता महाराष्ट्रस्य राज्यस्य मुद्रा भद्राय राजते (The glory of this Seal of Maharashtra will grow like the first day moon. It will be worshiped by the world and will shine only for the well being of people) Recently Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (Mns) Party Chief Raj Thackeray On Thursday Unveiled His Party's New Saffron-coloured Flag Bearing The Octagonal 'Rajmudra' (Royal Seal) Of Chhatrapati Shivaji At Its Centre.
